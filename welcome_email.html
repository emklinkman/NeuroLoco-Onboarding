  3. Complete PEERRS training

    The Human Subjects Research Protections course is mandatory if you will be working on a research project where interaction with human subjects or analysis of human subjects data will be taking place. The training should take no more than 3 hours to complete. Let your mentor or project supervisor know once you have completed the course.

    If you’re not sure if you need to complete the course based on your assigned duties, reach out to your mentor or project supervisor for clarification.

  4. Check out Lab documentation

    Vicon SOPs - Vicon MoCap operation and troubleshooting
    Delsys SOPs - Delsys EMG operation and troubleshooting
